How To Play

🏎️

The Objective

You are shown F1 drivers one at a time. For each driver, assign them to a category on your bingo board. Your goal is to correctly fill as many of the 15 categories as possible before you run out of drivers.

📋

The Board

Your board has 15 category cells — stats, nationalities, teams, decades, and more. Each game generates a fresh random set of categories, so no two games are the same.

⏱️

The Timer

You have 15 seconds per driver. If the timer runs out before you assign them, they are skipped and you move on to the next driver. You can also skip manually at any time.

✅

Assigning a Driver

Click any open category cell to assign the current driver to it. If the driver matches the category, it counts as correct. If not, it counts as wrong — and that cell is locked. You only get one shot per cell.

🃏

The Driver Pool

Each game draws 40 drivers from the full roster. The pool is guaranteed to contain at least one valid answer for every category on your board — so every category is always winnable.

🏆

Scoring

Your final score is the number of categories you answered correctly out of 15. Your total time is also tracked — in multiplayer, time is used as a tiebreaker when two players have the same score.

👥

Multiplayer

Create a private room and share the code or link with friends. Everyone plays the same 40 drivers in the same order, but each player gets their own randomly generated categories. First to finish doesn't win — most correct answers does.
